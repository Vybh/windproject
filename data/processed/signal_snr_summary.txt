SIGNAL SNR ANALYSIS: DE_ball vs FE_ball
=================================================================

Metric                          DE_ball     FE_ball     DE/FE Ratio
-----------------------------------------------------------------
RMS Energy                     0.133646    0.164006        0.81×
SNR at BSF                         0.08      304.75        0.00×
Spectral Peak Prominence           0.93        1.34        0.69×
Attenuation                        -1.8 dB

Fault frequency analysed: BSF (Ball Spin Frequency)
BSF = 2.357 × shaft_speed = 70.6 Hz  (at shaft_speed = 29.95 Hz)

Conclusion:
  DE_ball signals are 0.8× stronger (RMS) than FE_ball signals.
  This -1.8 dB path attenuation is the primary reason FE_ball
  is the lowest-performing fault class across all ML models.
